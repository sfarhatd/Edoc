What is eDoc?

eDoc is a small kids' health education enterprise created by two junior doctors, Roshni and Sara.

This eDoc website aims to provide a fun and interactive way for kids to learn about how their bodies work and what happens when they get ill. Through jargon- busting and a better understanding of what doctors do, eDoc hopes to reduce the unknown for children coming to hospital, and therefore reduce how scary hospital can be for kids.
